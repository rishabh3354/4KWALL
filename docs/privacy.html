Privacy Policy — 4KWALL

Last updated: 14 July 2026

4KWALL does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information. There is no analytics, no telemetry, no advertising, and no user account. Everything the app stores about you stays on your own computer.

4KWALL is a desktop wallpaper browser, downloader and auto-changer developed by Warlord Softwares (“we”, “us”). This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data.

Data stored on your device

The app stores the following locally, and only locally:

  • Settings and preferences — theme, wallpaper-change interval, monitor configuration, download folder.
  • Favorites and history — the wallpapers you favorite and view, kept in a local database.
  • Cached thumbnails — temporary images that make browsing faster.
  • Downloaded wallpapers — saved to a folder you choose (by default, your Pictures folder).
  • License key (Pro only) — stored on your device so the app can unlock Pro features.

None of this is uploaded to us. You can remove all of it by uninstalling the app and deleting its data folder.

Network connections

4KWALL connects to the internet only for these purposes:

  • Wallhaven (wallhaven.cc) — to search for, preview and download wallpapers. Wallhaven receives your IP address and standard HTTP request information, exactly as it would if you visited their website. That data is handled under Wallhaven’s privacy policy.

Third-party content

Wallpapers shown in the app are supplied by Wallhaven, not by us. 4KWALL requests only content marked as suitable for general audiences. We do not host or moderate that content.

Children

4KWALL is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from them.

Permissions

On Windows, 4KWALL runs with standard user permissions so that it can set your desktop wallpaper and save downloaded images to a folder you choose. It does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, location, or documents.
